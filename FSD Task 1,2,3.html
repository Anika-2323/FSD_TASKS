ABOUT

Welcome to Demon School! Iruma-kun (Mairimashita! Iruma-kun) is a highly popular manga and anime series created by Osamu Nishi. It follows Iruma Suzuki, a kind-hearted 14-year-old human who is sold to a demon by his irresponsible parents. Instead of eating him, the powerful demon Sullivan adopts him as his grandson and enrolls him in the Netherworld's Babyls School for Demons.
